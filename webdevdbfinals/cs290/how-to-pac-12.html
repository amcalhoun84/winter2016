Pac-12 Map and Custom Icons

This is a rather simple use of markers to show the location of each of the PAC-12 Universities. In addition, I have used custom icons to make each university stand out. The process itself it actually rather straight-forward and can be quickly duplicated once you successfully created one marker. A lot of the leg work has to do with figuring out of the latitudinal and longitudinal coordinates. I also created a large number of locations, with meaningful names to make them easier to remember and create consistency in the script. Remember, that the two most important parts of the marker functions are the map and position properties.

The map attaches the marker to a particular map, in this case, mapPAC12, and the position is the location variable that is defined earlier. This is much cleaner than putting the latitude and longitude in each time.
